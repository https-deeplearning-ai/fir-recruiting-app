## Business Value

### For Recruiters:
1. **Identify Talent Pools**: Find companies with relevant experience in your target domain
2. **Competitive Intelligence**: Understand the competitive landscape for sourcing
3. **Targeted Outreach**: Focus on companies most similar to your hiring profile

### For Hiring Managers:
1. **Market Research**: Discover competitors and adjacent companies automatically
2. **Talent Benchmarking**: See which companies have expertise in your domain
3. **Strategic Hiring**: Prioritize companies for targeted recruiting campaigns

## Technical Highlights

### AI Models Used:
- **Claude Sonnet 4.5**: JD parsing (temp 0.2), deep research (temp 0.1)
- **GPT-4o-mini**: Fast batch screening (20 companies/batch)

### APIs Integrated:
- **Tavily Search**: Web search for company discovery
- **Supabase**: Session storage and results persistence

### Real-time Architecture:
- **SSE (Server-Sent Events)**: Live progress streaming
- **Async Python**: Parallel processing for speed
- **React Frontend**: Real-time UI updates

## Example Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Voice AI Startup Recruiting
**Input**: JD for "Senior Voice AI Engineer" mentioning Deepgram, AssemblyAI
**Output**: 22 companies including direct competitors (Speechmatics), adjacent companies (ElevenLabs, Otter.ai), and talent pools

### Use Case 2: Fintech Company Search
**Input**: JD for "Blockchain Engineer" mentioning Coinbase, Ripple
**Output**: Cryptocurrency companies, DeFi protocols, blockchain infrastructure companies

### Use Case 3: No Companies Mentioned
**Input**: JD for "ML Engineer" in "healthcare AI" domain
**Output**: Discovers companies via web search (Tempus, PathAI, Paige.AI) even without explicit mentions
